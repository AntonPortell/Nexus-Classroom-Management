# NexMang: Nexus Classroom Management Software

Welcome to NexMang, your companion in enhancing your student learning experience. NexMang is a Java software (MacOS & WindowsOS) that offers numerous benefits to users, both students and teachers, in managing their heavy and busy timetables and classroom reservations more effectively.

## Features

 * Account generation: Allows for a unique experience for all students.
 * Timetable: Upload YOUR timetable and keep it safe and easily accessible from your homepage
 * Search Engine: Search for your classes, classrooms and their corresponding teachers using the code given in your timetable!
 * Map: Allows for easy, intuitive navigation between classes in a relatively confusing building.
 * 'Starter Pack' Document: Includes details for students, such as what is served each day at the canteen, or just where the canteen is in general!
 * Resources: Explore A wealth of resources on mental health, including articles, videos, and guides.
 * Emergency Contacts: Quickly find contact information for mental health support and emergency services.

*For Teachers and Admins*:
 * Reservations: Reserve Classrooms for any period of time so that your colleagues will never again confuse classes with you!
 * Edit Class Info: Add, delete and update Class information DB throughout the year.
 * Authentication: Make sure only authorised users can access these features!

## How to Use

Download the software and it should already be connected to the database if your PC is connected to the school wifi! New Users can simply create a new account (using ONLY their Nexus email), while existing users can just sign in! Follow the instructions for creating a new username and password and uploading your timetable. It's that easy!
